Learning Plan Part 1: Foundations

Foundations Learning Plan

Long Term Goal or Career Pathway:
My long-term goal is to develop and sell apps that enhance user experiences and address market needs. In the medium term, I aim to secure part-time employment as a full-stack web developer. My immediate goal is to relaunch my own business website using my own code and hosting, creating a platform that not only showcases my skills but also opens up new business opportunities and provides a superior experience for my clients.

Strengths and Limitations in Learning:
I am highly motivated and engaged when I find the subject matter intriguing, capable of immersing myself for hours to enhance my skills and perfect my processes. However, as a self-employed freelancer, my main challenge is maintaining discipline. I often struggle to stay on task when I’m not intrinsically motivated to work.

Skills Development:
I am eager to develop a more consistent 9-5 work ethic at Dev Academy. Given my background as a freelancer, working within a structured schedule will be a valuable skill as I transition into more collaborative environments. I also look forward to enhancing my teamwork abilities, which have not been a focus in my recent professional experiences.

Managing Workload:
I commit to actively managing my workload and ensuring productive and safe interactions with peers, facilitators, and industry/community representatives. I recognize that staying ahead of my tasks is crucial for my mental well-being and effectiveness. My strategy is to avoid procrastination at all costs—considering myself “behind” if I’m merely on time, and “on time” if I’m ahead.

Handling Stress and Overload:
When I feel overwhelmed, I will take proactive steps to manage stress, such as going for a 30-minute run at the gym next door. This physical activity has proven effective in managing my stress and will be an integral part of my routine.

Seeking Help:
I vow to improve how I seek assistance, particularly from classmates and facilitators. I will not hesitate to ask for help when needed, ensuring that I address challenges before they become overwhelming.

Expectations from the Facilitation Team:
I expect the facilitation team to create a supportive and inclusive environment where I can safely make mistakes and learn from them. Their guidance will be crucial as I navigate new concepts and strive to apply them effectively.

Scheduling and Commitments:
I have minimal external commitments, except for occasional meetings at 7pm and one scheduled Friday off towards the end of the course for a job commitment. While there is a possibility of needing to attend to a short-term shoot during the main bootcamp, my primary focus and commitment will be to my learning and development. I plan to keep my weekends free to ensure I have ample time for rest and recovery.

Learning Plan Part 2: Bootcamp

Bootcamp Plan

Long-term Goals and Career Pathway:
Redesign my business website from the ground up, move away from Squarespace, and integrate custom tools for work processes, client sign-ups, and customer interactions. Learn Swift and SwiftUI to develop iOS apps and consider full or part-time employment opportunities.

Skills to Develop During Bootcamp:
Focus on learning how to implement a sign-in process, manage a backend database, and create visually appealing animations. Continue to develop coding skills and learn new programming languages.

Strengths and Limitations:
Strengths: Proficient in public speaking and a keen interest in technical challenges.
Limitations: Managing a heavy workload and staying focused for extended periods.

Non-Technical Challenges:
Primary challenges include time management and working effectively with others. Public speaking is a strength, but teamwork and human connectivity are areas for development.

Development of Human Skills:
Enhance teamwork capabilities and general interpersonal relations to build meaningful connections and friendships during Bootcamp.

Expectations from Bootcamp Team:
Seek clear and thorough explanations of complex concepts to ensure a deep understanding of course materials.

Personal Expectations and Accountability:
Commit to arriving on time, fulfilling responsibilities, and completing all assigned work both independently and collaboratively.

Seeking Help:
Utilize resources like ChatGPT for assistance with coding problems and conceptual clarifications.

Commitment Statement:
I commit to giving all aspects of the Bootcamp, especially the human skills segments, a fair chance despite my apprehensions. I trust that the course framework is well-designed to enhance my professional and personal growth.
