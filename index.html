Welcome to my journey through full-stack web development at Dev Academy!

Over the next 17 weeks, I’ll be diving headfirst into the intricate world of coding, exploring the layers of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and beyond. This blog is not just a repository of what I learn, but a living document of my progression, an exercise assigned to merge practice with theory. As I have spent the last decade crafting websites on platforms like Squarespace for my buisness as a freelance videogrpaher, the shift from visual site-building to understanding the raw code that powers such platforms is exhilarating.
This blog marks the beginning of my journey ‘under the hood’ of web technologies. Designed for myself, my fellow students and great minds at Dev Academy, this space will serve as a shared learning experience, reflecting on the challenges and breakthroughs of transitioning from a visual to a code-centric approach in web development.

Bookmark this page for easy access to follow my journey.
